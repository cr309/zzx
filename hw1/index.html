Homework 1: Prokudin-Gorskii Colorization

Project Introduction

Building on the first assignment, this work adds coarse-to-fine Gaussian pyramid alignment, Gray-World white balancing, automatic contrast stretching and variance-based black-border cropping. All 14 official images were processed in under 61 s and are accompanied by exact offset vectors and before/after comparisons.
