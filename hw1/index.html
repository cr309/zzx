Homework 1: Prokudin-Gorskii Colorization

Project Introduction

Building on the first assignment, this work adds coarse-to-fine Gaussian pyramid alignment, Gray-World white balancing, automatic contrast stretching and variance-based black-border cropping. All official and self-selected images were processed in under one minute and are accompanied by offset vectors and before/after comparisons.
